Alla Diavola Butter

Prep: 15 min
Ready: 1 hr 15 min
1 cup
Condiments Gluten-Free Quick Spicy Vegetarian

This fiery compound butter draws inspiration from Italian "alla diavola" dishes, which translates to "devil style" - meaning deliciously spicy. Featuring multiple layers of heat from smoked paprika, chile flakes, black pepper, and pepperoncini, this versatile butter can be adjusted to suit your preferred spice level. Perfect for melting over steaks, spreading on bread, tossing with pasta, and more.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp dried chile flakes
  • 1 tbsp cracked black pepper
  • ½ tsp kosher salt
  • ¼ cup finely chopped seeded pepperoncini (patted dry on paper towels after chopping)
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce, such as Tabasco

Instructions

  1. Fold all the ingredients together with a wooden spoon or rubber spatula and pile into whatever container you want to serve or save it in.
  2. Chill the butter for at least 1 hour to firm it up and to let the flavors marry and permeate the butter.
